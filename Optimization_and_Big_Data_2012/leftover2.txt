<h2>Edinburgh</h2>

<!-- <img src="imgs/640px-Edinburgh_Overview_Salisbury_Crag.jpg"> -->

<p>To quote <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edinburgh">Wikipedia</a>:</p>
<p>Edinburgh is the capital city of Scotland, [...] the Athens of the North. The Old Town and New Town districts of Edinburgh were listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1995. [...] Edinburgh attracts over 1 million overseas visitors a year, making it the second most visited tourist destination in the United Kingdom.</p>

<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edinburgh_Airport" title="Edinburgh Airport">Edinburgh Airport</a> is Scotland's busiest airport and principal international gateway to the capital. [...] <a href="/wiki/Edinburgh_Waverley_railway_station" title="Edinburgh Waverley railway station">Edinburgh Waverley</a> is the primary railway station serving the city. Waverley serves as the terminus for trains arriving from <a href="/wiki/London_King%27s_Cross_railway_station" title="London King's Cross railway station">London King's Cross</a>.</p>
