Workshop Venue

The workshop is held on May 1-2, 2013, in the Informatics Forum (a building housing the School of Informatics) in the George Square campus of the University of Edinburgh, in Edinburgh's very center. Address: 10 Crichton Street.

 




Informatics Forum is building #28 on page 3 of the University of Edinburgh Campus Map (pdf).

Colloquium Venue

The Colloquium is held in the James Clerk Maxwell Building (JCMB) of the King's Buildings campus of the University of Edinburgh, in the southern part of the city. After a coffee and tea in 5th floor common room (the room on the left when getting off the elevator) at 15:30, the lecture starts in Lecture Theatre C at 16:10 (on the third floor of JCMB) and will last 50 minutes.

 

The colloquium lecture will be followed by a Best Poster Award Ceremony (17:00-17:15) in Lecture Theatre C, and a Networking Reception in the Costa Coffee area (third floor of JCMB; 17:15-19:00; refreshments provided).




James Clerk Maxwell Building is building #13 in the King's Buildings campus onpage 4 of the University of Edinburgh Campus Maps (pdf).
